Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

TENSURA WebBooks respects your privacy. This website is a web novel platform that allows users to read stories and optionally sign in to comment.

Information We Collect

  • Basic account information (such as name or email) when you sign in using third-party providers like Google, GitHub, Facebook, or Microsoft.
  • Comments you voluntarily post on chapters.
  • Anonymous analytics such as chapter view counts.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only to:

  • Authenticate users
  • Display comments
  • Track chapter views

Data Storage

All data is securely handled using Firebase services. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Third-Party Authentication

This site uses third-party login providers (Google, GitHub, Facebook, Microsoft). Authentication is handled by those providers according to their privacy policies.

Contact

If you have any questions, contact the site administrator.
