Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Overview

At TENSURA WebBooks, we respect your right to control your personal data. This page provides comprehensive instructions on how to request deletion of your data from our platform. We are committed to processing all deletion requests promptly and in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations.

This page is also provided to comply with Facebook's Platform Policy requirements for apps that use Facebook Login.

What Data Can Be Deleted?

When you request account deletion, the following data associated with your account will be permanently removed:

Personal Information

  • Email address
  • Display name/username
  • Profile picture (if provided via social login)
  • Account credentials and authentication tokens

Activity Data

  • Reading history and progress
  • Bookmarks and favorites
  • Comments you've posted on chapters
  • View tracking data associated with your account
  • Preferences and settings

Technical Data

  • Session data and authentication tokens
  • Device identifiers associated with your account
  • Login history

Data Retention After Deletion

Please note the following regarding data retention:

Immediately Deleted

  • Your account will be immediately deactivated and you will be logged out
  • Your personal identifiable information will be removed from our active databases
  • Your ability to log in will be permanently revoked

Retained for Limited Period

  • Backup Systems: Data may remain in backup systems for up to 30 days for disaster recovery purposes, after which it will be permanently deleted
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention, financial records)
  • Aggregated Analytics: Anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you may be retained for statistical purposes

Public Content

  • Comments: Your public comments will be anonymized (display name changed to "Deleted User") but may remain visible to maintain discussion continuity. You can request complete removal of specific comments before account deletion.

How to Request Data Deletion

Method 1: Self-Service Account Deletion (Recommended)

Follow these steps to delete your account yourself:

  1. Log in to your TENSURA WebBooks account
  2. Click on your profile or the menu icon in the top-right corner
  3. Navigate to "Account Settings" or "Profile Settings"
  4. Scroll down to the "Delete Account" section
  5. Click the "Delete My Account" button
  6. Confirm your decision by re-entering your password or confirming through authentication
  7. Click "Confirm Deletion" to permanently delete your account

Note: This action is immediate and irreversible. All your data will be permanently deleted.

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access your account or prefer manual assistance:

Send an email to: delete@tensurawebbooks.com

Include the following information in your email:

  • Subject Line: "Data Deletion Request - [Your Email]"
  • Email Address: The email associated with your account
  • Account Verification: Answer one of the following:
    • Last chapter you read
    • Approximate account creation date
    • Login method used (email, Google, Facebook)
  • Request Statement: "I request the permanent deletion of my account and all associated personal data from TENSURA WebBooks."

Processing Time: We will process your request within 30 days of verification. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

Method 3: Facebook Data Deletion Request

If you signed in using Facebook:

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings
  2. Navigate to "Apps and Websites"
  3. Find "TENSURA WebBooks" in the list
  4. Click "Remove" to revoke Facebook access
  5. To delete your TENSURA WebBooks account data, follow Method 1 or Method 2 above

Important: Removing the app from Facebook only disconnects the app from your Facebook account. To delete your data from TENSURA WebBooks, you must also request account deletion using one of the methods above.

Method 4: Contact Form

Visit our contact form at: tensurawebbooks.com/contact

  • Select "Data Deletion Request" from the subject dropdown
  • Provide your account email and verification information
  • Submit the form

What Happens After You Request Deletion?

Step 1: Request Received

We receive your deletion request and send an acknowledgment email within 24-48 hours.

Step 2: Identity Verification

We verify your identity to ensure the request is legitimate and protect against unauthorized deletions.

Step 3: Data Deletion

Your account is deactivated and your data is permanently deleted from our active systems within 7 days.

Step 4: Backup Cleanup

Data is removed from backup systems within 30 days of the initial request.

Step 5: Confirmation

You receive a final confirmation email once the deletion process is complete.

Before You Delete Your Account

⚠️ Important Considerations

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Before proceeding, please consider:

  • Data Loss: All your reading progress, bookmarks, and comments will be permanently lost
  • No Recovery: Once deleted, you cannot recover your account or data
  • Re-registration: You can create a new account with the same email, but it will be a fresh start with no previous data
  • Public Comments: Your comments may remain visible but will be anonymized
  • Alternative Options: Consider deactivating your account temporarily instead of permanent deletion

Alternative: Account Deactivation

If you're not sure about permanent deletion, you can deactivate your account temporarily:

  • Your account will be hidden and inaccessible
  • You can reactivate it at any time by logging back in
  • Your data will be preserved during deactivation
  • To deactivate, contact us at support@tensurawebbooks.com

Your Rights Under Data Protection Laws

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights:

GDPR (European Users)

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

CCPA (California Users)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising rights

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does the deletion process take?

A: Account deactivation is immediate. Complete data deletion from all systems (including backups) takes up to 30 days.

Q: Can I recover my account after deletion?

A: No. Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All data is permanently lost.

Q: What happens to my comments?

A: Comments are anonymized (username changed to "Deleted User") but remain visible to preserve discussion context. You can request complete removal before deletion.

Q: Can I use the same email to create a new account?

A: Yes, after deletion, you can create a new account with the same email, but it will be a completely new account with no previous data.

Q: Will deleting my TENSURA account also delete my Google/Facebook account?

A: No. Deleting your TENSURA account only removes data from our platform. Your social media accounts remain unaffected.

Q: I can't access my account. How do I delete it?

A: Use Method 2 (Email Request) above. We'll verify your identity through security questions and process the deletion manually.

Q: Is there a waiting period before deletion?

A: For self-service deletion, the process is immediate. For email requests, we verify your identity first (1-3 days), then proceed with deletion.

Q: What if I change my mind?

A: Once deletion is confirmed, it cannot be reversed. Consider account deactivation if you're unsure.
