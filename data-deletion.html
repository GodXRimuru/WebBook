Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data from TENSURA WebBooks, follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to the site administrator requesting data deletion.
  2. Include the email address you used to log in.
  3. Your account data and comments will be permanently deleted within a reasonable timeframe.

Authentication data is managed by third-party providers (Google, GitHub, Facebook, Microsoft). You may also remove access directly from your provider’s account settings.
